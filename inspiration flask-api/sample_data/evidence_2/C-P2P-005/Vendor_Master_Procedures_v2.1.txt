VENDOR MASTER DATA MANAGEMENT PROCEDURES
Version: 2.1 | Effective: October 2023 | Owner: Vendor Management Lead

1. QUARTERLY REVIEW
   - Full vendor master extract generated by IT within 3 business days of quarter-end
   - Vendor Management team reviews all active records for accuracy
   - Changes verified independently (banking details require call-back)
   - Review workpaper signed off by Vendor Management Lead and Procurement Manager

2. BANKING DETAIL CHANGES
   - Any change to vendor banking details must be verified via independent call-back
   - Call-back must be to a phone number obtained independently (not from the change request)
   - Call log must document: date, time, bank representative name, verification confirmation

3. DUPLICATE DETECTION
   - IT runs duplicate detection report quarterly (matching on name, tax ID, address)
   - Duplicates reviewed and merged or justified by Vendor Management team

4. REVIEW TIMELINE
   Target: Complete review within 10 business days of quarter-end.
   (Note: This is a target, not a formal SLA — to be formalized in next version.)
